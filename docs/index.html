We are making crypto greener ! ...

When we are talking about the carbon footprint of bitcoin and to a lesser level ethereum, it seems irresponsible to allow such inefficient technology to exists.

However I really believe blockchain can have tremendous benefits to society.
I whish bitcoin didn't choose proof-of-work as a consensus protocol.

While PoW can make sense for an international DAO to decide about cross continental contract, or vote for an enact a new international law; for buying a pizza do we really need the world to know and the world to validate and approve the transaction ?

PoW might very much be an overkill.

Still Bitcoin is the most popular crypto-currency and we have to align with the choice of the majority (isn't it what democraty is about).
So what can we do with "mined" bitcoins ?

  1. first make sure we use renewable for powering the mining nodes
  2. account for all emission and waste generate during the creation of the coinbase transaction.
  3. offset the bitcoin with the appropriate audited carbon removal credit.
  4. wrap the bitcoin and issue a carbon zero certificate
  5. the wrapped bitcoin can be used in place of the bitcoin for any crypto purchase, or hodling its value.
Our first website is online, learn more about the project : https://carbonzero.network/.
